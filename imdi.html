Our Journey

From a small workshop in Punjab to becoming one of India's trusted sewing machine manufacturers.

Founded in 1969, Vimmi Sewing Machine began as a humble workshop in Punjab with a vision to make quality sewing accessible to every household and business. Over the decades, we've combined traditional craftsmanship with modern engineering to create machines that stand the test of time.
